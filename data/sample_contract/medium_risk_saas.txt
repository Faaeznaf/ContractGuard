                    SAAS SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT


This SaaS Subscription Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of 
the 1st day of June, 2024 (the "Effective Date"), by and between:

     Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation with its principal place of 
     business at 789 Business Boulevard, New York, NY 10001 (hereinafter 
     referred to as "Customer" or "Subscriber"),

and

     DataAnalytics SaaS Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal 
     place of business at 321 Software Street, San Jose, CA 95110 
     (hereinafter referred to as "Provider" or "Company").

Customer and Provider are collectively referred to herein as the "Parties" 
and individually as a "Party."


                              RECITALS

WHEREAS, Provider offers a cloud-based data analytics platform and related 
services; and

WHEREAS, Customer desires to subscribe to and use Provider's software 
platform pursuant to the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements 
contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the 
receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties 
agree as follows:


1. SUBSCRIPTION SERVICES

   1.1 Grant of License. Subject to the terms and conditions of this 
       Agreement, Provider grants to Customer a non-exclusive, 
       non-transferable, non-sublicensable right to access and use the 
       Provider's data analytics platform (the "Platform") and related 
       services (collectively, the "Services") during the Subscription 
       Term solely for Customer's internal business purposes.

   1.2 Service Description. The Services include access to Provider's 
       proprietary data analytics software, data visualization tools, 
       reporting features, API access, technical support, and software 
       updates and maintenance as described in Exhibit A attached hereto.

   1.3 User Accounts. Customer may create user accounts for its authorized 
       employees and contractors (each an "Authorized User") up to the 
       number of user licenses purchased. Customer is responsible for all 
       activities conducted under its user accounts and shall ensure that 
       all Authorized Users comply with this Agreement.

   1.4 Restrictions. Customer shall not, and shall not permit any third 
       party to: (a) reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the 
       Platform; (b) copy, modify, or create derivative works of the 
       Services; (c) sublicense, resell, or distribute the Services; 
       (d) use the Services for competitive analysis or benchmarking; or 
       (e) use the Services in any manner that violates applicable law.


2. SUBSCRIPTION TERM AND RENEWAL

   2.1 Initial Term. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date 
       and shall continue for an initial term of two (2) years (the 
       "Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with the 
       provisions herein.

   2.2 Automatic Renewal. Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this 
       Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year 
       terms (each a "Renewal Term") unless either Party provides written 
       notice of non-renewal at least one hundred twenty (120) days prior 
       to the end of the then-current term. The Initial Term and any 
       Renewal Terms are collectively referred to as the "Subscription 
       Term."

   2.3 Termination for Convenience. Either Party may terminate this 
       Agreement for convenience upon providing written notice at least 
       one hundred twenty (120) days prior to the desired termination date.

   2.4 Termination for Cause. Either Party may terminate this Agreement 
       immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially 
       breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such 
       breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice 
       specifying the breach.

   2.5 Effect of Termination. Upon termination or expiration of this 
       Agreement, Customer's right to access and use the Services shall 
       immediately cease. Customer shall pay all outstanding fees through 
       the effective date of termination. Provider shall, upon Customer's 
       written request made within thirty (30) days of termination, make 
       Customer's data available for export in a standard format.


3. FEES AND PAYMENT

   3.1 Subscription Fees. Customer shall pay Provider the subscription 
       fees set forth in Exhibit B attached hereto (the "Fees"). Fees are 
       based on the subscription tier, number of user licenses, data 
       volume, and any additional features or services purchased.

   3.2 Payment Terms. Provider shall invoice Customer monthly or annually 
       as selected by Customer. All invoices shall be paid within forty-
       five (45) days of the invoice date (Net 45). Late payments shall 
       accrue interest at a rate of one percent (1%) per month or the 
       maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.

   3.3 Fee Adjustments. Provider may increase the Fees upon renewal by 
       providing Customer with written notice at least ninety (90) days 
       prior to the renewal date. If Customer does not agree to the 
       increased Fees, Customer may terminate this Agreement pursuant to 
       Section 2.2.

   3.4 Taxes. Customer shall be responsible for all applicable federal, 
       state, local, and foreign taxes, duties, tariffs, and similar 
       assessments (excluding taxes based on Provider's net income).


4. DATA RIGHTS AND USAGE

   4.1 Customer Data. As between the Parties, Customer retains all right, 
       title, and interest in and to all data, information, and content 
       uploaded, submitted, or transmitted by Customer to the Platform 
       ("Customer Data"). Customer grants Provider a limited, worldwide, 
       non-exclusive license to host, copy, transmit, display, and process 
       Customer Data solely to the extent necessary to provide the 
       Services.

   4.2 Data Usage Rights. Customer acknowledges and agrees that Provider 
       may collect, analyze, and use aggregated and anonymized usage data 
       derived from Customer's use of the Services (including statistical 
       data, performance metrics, and usage patterns) for Provider's 
       business purposes, including to improve and enhance the Services, 
       develop new features and products, conduct market research and 
       analytics, and for benchmarking purposes. Provider may also use 
       such aggregated and anonymized data to create industry reports, 
       trend analyses, and other derivative works, provided that such data 
       cannot be used to identify Customer or any individual.

   4.3 Data Security. Provider shall implement and maintain reasonable 
       administrative, physical, and technical safeguards designed to 
       protect Customer Data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. 
       Provider's security practices are described in its Security Policy 
       available at Provider's website.

   4.4 Data Backup. Provider shall perform regular backups of Customer 
       Data in accordance with its standard backup procedures. However, 
       Customer is solely responsible for maintaining independent backups 
       of Customer Data.


5. CONFIDENTIALITY

   5.1 Confidential Information. Each Party acknowledges that it may 
       receive or have access to confidential and proprietary information 
       of the other Party, including but not limited to technical data, 
       trade secrets, business plans, customer information, financial 
       information, product roadmaps, and other non-public information 
       (collectively, "Confidential Information"). The terms and pricing 
       of this Agreement shall be deemed Confidential Information of both 
       Parties.

   5.2 Confidentiality Obligations. Each Party agrees to: (a) maintain the 
       confidentiality of the other Party's Confidential Information using 
       at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own 
       confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care; 
       (b) not disclose such Confidential Information to any third party 
       without the prior written consent of the disclosing Party; and 
       (c) use such Confidential Information solely for the purposes 
       contemplated by this Agreement. These confidentiality obligations 
       shall survive for a period of three (3) years following the 
       termination or expiration of this Agreement.

   5.3 Exceptions. Confidential Information shall not include information 
       that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of 
       this Agreement by the receiving Party; (b) is rightfully received 
       by the receiving Party from a third party without breach of any 
       confidentiality obligation; (c) was independently developed by the 
       receiving Party without use of or reference to the Confidential 
       Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, 
       or court order, provided that the receiving Party provides prompt 
       written notice to the disclosing Party.
